Company Overview

Bokhutlo Trading and Enterprise (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic and diversified company providing professional cleaning, farming, construction, and catering solutions. Founded by Mary Baitsiwe, the company is dedicated to delivering high-quality services and products to schools, hospitals, businesses, households, and institutions.

Although currently operating from a home-based facility, Bokhutlo Trading and Enterprise is driven by efficiency, reliability, and a commitment to excellence, ensuring clients receive consistent value and superior results.

📈

Business Model

The company operates through a direct order-and-delivery model, enabling quick turnaround times and efficient service delivery. This approach allows us to be responsive to client needs while maintaining high standards of quality.

As financial capacity grows, plans are in place to establish a dedicated facility to enhance accessibility, streamline operations, and improve customer experience.

Mary Baitsiwe established Bokhutlo Trading and Enterprise with a vision to provide integrated solutions that transform spaces, build futures, and nurture growth. Her leadership and commitment to excellence have been the driving force behind the company's success.

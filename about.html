Our Farming Philosophy

Bokhutlo Farming Enterprise is a dedicated agricultural company that believes in sustainable farming practices that honor the land while producing high-quality food. Founded by Mary Baitsiwe, our farm combines traditional farming wisdom with modern techniques to create a model of responsible agriculture.

We operate from our family-owned land in Kome Village, where we've cultivated not just crops, but relationships with our community and respect for our environment.

Our Approach

We practice a holistic approach to farming that considers the entire ecosystem. Our methods prioritize soil health, water conservation, and biodiversity while maintaining productive yields.

As our resources grow, we're committed to expanding our sustainable practices and investing in technologies that will help us farm more efficiently while reducing our environmental footprint.

Our Farming Journey

The milestones that have shaped our growth

The Beginning

Started with a small plot of family land, focusing on subsistence farming and local market sales.

2015

Livestock Integration

Introduced cattle and poultry to create a more diversified and sustainable farming system.

2018

Formal Enterprise

Registered as Bokhutlo Farming Enterprise (Pty) Ltd and began supplying to regional markets.

2020

Sustainable Expansion

Implemented irrigation systems and began practicing crop rotation and soil conservation techniques.

2023

Mary Baitsiwe

Founder & Head Farmer

Mary Baitsiwe established Bokhutlo Farming Enterprise with a deep respect for the land passed down through generations. Growing up in a farming family, she learned the importance of sustainable practices and community support long before these became mainstream concepts.

Her vision extends beyond profit to creating a farming model that can be sustained for generations, providing not just food but also employment and education for her community. Under her leadership, Bokhutlo has become a symbol of what small-scale, sustainable farming can achieve.

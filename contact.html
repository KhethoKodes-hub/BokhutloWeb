Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve clients in and around Kome Village and the surrounding regions. As we expand our operations, we're working to extend our service area across South Africa.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide complimentary estimates for all our services. Contact us with your project details, and we'll prepare a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

How quickly can you start a project?

For cleaning services, we can typically begin within 1-3 business days. Construction and larger projects may require more detailed planning. We'll provide a timeline during our consultation.

Are your staff trained and insured?

Yes, we maintain a professional, well-trained workforce capable of servicing complex environments. We prioritize safety and quality in all our operations.

Can you handle large commercial projects?

Absolutely! We have experience with hospitals, schools, corporate offices, and industrial facilities. Our team is equipped to handle projects of various scales and complexities.
